The Los Santos Sheriff's Department (LSSD)

The Los Santos Sheriff's Department (LSSD) was officially established in 1856. It has since been a cornerstone of public safety and law enforcement, adapting to the growing needs of Los Santos and its surrounding areas while upholding its core values of integrity, professionalism, and service to the community.

Gang Intelligence Unit

Investigates gang-related activities, tracks criminal organizations, and prevents gang violence.
